About Us

Old Lou’s Ragtime Band is a traditional jazz band from Louisville, Kentucky. Formed in 2018, the band is a congenial partnership of U of L music school grads and traditional jazz veterans, reviving the energy and spontaneous feel of early jazz. They strive to authentically replicate the sounds of early New Orleans jazz musicians such as Louis Armstrong, Jelly Roll Morton, Kid Ory, Joe “King” Oliver, and others. The group's instrumentation is typically clarinet, trumpet, trombone, tuba, banjo, and occasionally drums/washboard."

They are named after their tuba player's beloved Golden Retriever "Louie" aka "Old Lou". Old Lou’s Ragtime Band regularly performs at breweries, outdoor markets, community events, private parties, and weddings across the Kentuckiana region. You can hear them live in person, or online with Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube.
